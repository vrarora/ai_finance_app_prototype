## Design Context

### Users
Primary users are finance-anxious young earners in India who want help understanding where their money is going without feeling judged or overwhelmed. They are trying to spot waste, regain a sense of control, and make steady progress on near-term savings goals like an emergency fund.

### Brand Personality
Niva should feel calm, trustworthy, and supportive. The product should lower financial stress, explain tradeoffs clearly, and feel more like a steady guide than a high-energy fintech product.

### Aesthetic Direction
Use a warm, premium-light interface with quiet confidence rather than a futuristic neobank look. Typography should feel composed and human, with enough contrast to guide anxious users gently through insights. Avoid playful visual signals, aggressive product-marketing energy, and cold corporate styling.

### Design Principles
1. Reduce anxiety before showcasing intelligence.
2. Make financial information feel legible, steady, and grounded.
3. Use hierarchy to guide attention without visual noise.
4. Favor warmth and clarity over novelty or hype.
5. Keep the interface supportive, never performative.
